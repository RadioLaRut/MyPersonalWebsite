Project 04

Neon Rain

The objective was to balance high-contrast neon emissives with dense volumetric fog without losing detail in the shadows. Inspired by the cinematography of Blade Runner 2049, I focused on a dual-tone palette—cyan key lights fighting against magenta fill. The challenge lay in maintaining physically accurate light falloff while stylizing the scene for narrative impact.

Volumetrics

Exponential height fog was used to create depth. Localized volumetric point lights were placed near neon signs to simulate humidity scattering light.

Materials

Custom master materials for wet asphalt utilized vertex painting to blend between puddles and dry patches, affecting roughness maps dynamically.

Post-Process

Final color grading done in DaVinci Resolve. Applied a film grain overlay and chromatic aberration at the fringes to mimic anamorphic lens characteristics.
